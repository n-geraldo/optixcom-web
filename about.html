About Optixcom

Optixcom is an international telecommunication company with headquarters in Tirana, that for 20 years builds its own fiber-optic network and provides data and telecommunication services since 2005.

Our Specialization

We specialize in providing electronic communications services which includes Internet access services, maintaining computer networks and deploying security solutions. We are dedicated exclusively to business users to whom we can solve the communication problems of their companies.

Our Motivation

We strive to provide our customers with the highest quality service, reliable network and the shortest implementation time, so our team is dedicated to this goal. With the stable growth of the company, we are committed to our primary purpose, which is customer satisfaction.

Our Business Strategy

We are actively building our network, focusing on small, medium and large business customers that we offer innovative and solving solutions. We are focused on the EU regional market and non-EU countries with which we cooperate. The global market is our goal towards we are successfully moving. In our developing and expanding, we maintain accessibility to clients and create packages according to their requirements and needs. Quality, stability and affordability are our main principles.

Our Company Structure

  • Technical Department – This is the base of our company. With their outstanding technical expertise, they make sure our service is flawlessly and that our company runs smoothly. We have high-quality and up-to-date team of people whose specialty is information and communication technology and software solutions.
  • Finance Department – They are the ones in charge of financial related questions. They assist us in making responsible decisions and support us in our business activities.
  • Business Development Department – The team that is an essential ingredient to your success. They make sure you feel taken care of and have the best available backing for your business. Their activities provides the company an upstream business path, and include corporate sales, sales to small, medium and large businesses, responsibility for business customers and marketing. They are one of the first people you will get in touch with.
  • HR Department – The pillar of our company is our HR. Taking care of our employees is their daily task. Interpersonal relationships are their specialty and they also deal with the recruitment and employment. One of the most important department of our company that affects the business and performance of the target company.
  • Legal Department – Our legal department makes sure that all actions are in the accordance with the regulations. They resolve inquiries and give opinions on certain legal issues, participate in important decisions and monitor the development of the projects. They are in the communication with all existing departments and with clients as well.
